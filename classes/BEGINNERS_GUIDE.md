## ğŸ­ Frontend vs Backend

Let's imagine you're running a restaurant! ğŸ•

### ğŸ¨ **Frontend** (What customers see)
- This is what people SEE and CLICK
- The buttons, colors, pictures, and animations
- Like the dining room and menu in a restaurant

**Examples:**
- The buttons in a game
- The colors on a website
- How a character moves on screen
- What you click and touch

**Languages:** HTML, CSS, JavaScript

### âš™ï¸ **Backend** (What happens behind the scenes)
- This is the BRAIN of your program
- It does the thinking and remembering
- Like the kitchen in a restaurant

**Examples:**
- Saving your high score
- Remembering your username
- Checking if you won the game
- Storing your inventory items

**Languages:** Python, JavaScript, Lua

### ğŸ¤ They Work Together!
- Frontend: "Player clicked the jump button!"
- Backend: "Okay, make the character jump THIS high!"
- Frontend: "Got it, showing the jump animation!"

### For Your Roblox Game:
- **Frontend**: How your game looks, the characters, the world
- **Backend**: The game rules, scoring, what happens when you touch things
